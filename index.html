LL-Compiler

LL Compiler is a small Little Lisp Language compiler. Developed by Danil Tumaykin and Igor Dultsev at Intel WS2012 at NGU-Intel Lab.

Further documentation is available at LL-Compiler wiki at NSU-Intel Lab.
